Harshit Raja

Chess player

- India's 69th chess grandmaster.
- Represented India in World Youth Chess Championship U-14 in Greece in 2015.
- Honoured with 'Best Upcoming Player', in Maharashtra Chess League
- Became an International Master in 2017
- Silver medalist in U-13 National Chess Championship in 2014.
- Recipient of the Shiv Chhatrapati award in 2019.
